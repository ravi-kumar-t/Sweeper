📖 Our Story

Born from a Simple Frustration

Sweeper was founded in Jalandhar, Punjab, by a family who struggled to find reliable, trustworthy household help. We experienced firsthand the frustration of unverified maids, sudden absences, and no accountability — so we decided to fix it.

We started with a simple idea: what if finding a maid was as easy, reliable, and stress-free as ordering food online? What if every family could have a professionally trained, background-verified helper at an affordable monthly price?

Today, Sweeper serves 500+ families across Jalandhar with a team of 50+ verified maids. Our replacement guarantee, transparent pricing, and professional approach have made us the most trusted cleaning service in the region.

How We Verify Our Maids

Your safety is our top priority. Here's our rigorous 5-step verification process.

🪪

ID Verification

Government-issued ID (Aadhaar/PAN) is collected and verified for every maid.

📍

Address Check

We verify the permanent and current address of every staff member.

📞

Reference Check

We contact previous employers and verify their work history.

🎓

Training

All maids complete a professional cleaning and etiquette training program.

📊

Performance Monitoring

Monthly feedback reviews to ensure consistent quality for all our clients.
